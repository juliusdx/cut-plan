The honest directive

The abs are buried, not underdeveloped. At ~18–20% body fat, no amount of crunches will reveal them. The deficit is the lever.

The plan: 4–6 months of disciplined cutting. Lift hard to keep every gram of muscle. Cardio to widen the deficit. Lower abs visible at ~10–12% body fat — that's the destination, ~5–7 kg of fat from here.
The trap: aggressive deficits work for 3 weeks then crash you. Patient 0.5 kg/week loss preserves muscle and stays sustainable. Slow is fast.

  • Stop eating 3h before bed. Wake-up belly is often digestion
  • Test eliminations 10–14 days each: dairy, gluten, carbonation, sweeteners
